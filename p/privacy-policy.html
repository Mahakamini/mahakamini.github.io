Overview

Atur Duit respects your privacy. This application is ad-free and does not sell, rent, or share any user data. This Privacy Policy explains what information is accessed, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Android Permissions Used

This app requests the following permissions as declared in AndroidManifest.xml:

  • <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE" />
  • <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE" />
Note: On newer versions of Android (10/11+), external storage access is limited by Scoped Storage. The app only accesses user-approved files and complies with Google Play's data access policies.

Why We Request Storage Permission

The storage permissions are used solely for the following purposes (adjust if needed):

  • To allow users to import or open files stored on their device (e.g., documents, images, or backups).
  • To save files created by the app into a folder accessible to the user (e.g., export, backup, or download results).
  • To perform backup and restore functions explicitly requested by the user.

Access occurs only after the user grants permission and only for files necessary to perform these functions.

Data Collected

No personal data is automatically collected by this app. Any files or information used by the app remain stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them.

  • Data stored locally: Files that you create, edit, or import using the app.
  • Data not collected: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any personal identifiers for analytics or advertising purposes.

Crash Reports and Analytics

This app does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If a crash reporting service is added in the future, it will only collect anonymous technical data necessary to fix app issues, never personal information.

How We Use Data

All data and files you choose to create or import remain under your control and are only used to provide app functionality. We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party without your explicit consent.

Data Storage and Security

The app stores files locally on your device. We implement reasonable measures to protect your data, but since no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Data Sharing

This app does not share any user data with third parties for advertising, analytics, or marketing purposes. Data may be shared only if:

  • You explicitly request or consent to it, or
  • We are required by law to disclose specific information (e.g., a court order).

User Rights

  • Access & Control: You can view, move, or delete any files you created or imported using the app.
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke storage access permissions anytime in your device settings. Some app features may not work without it.
  • Delete Data: If you need help removing any locally stored data, please contact us via email.

Compliance with Google Play Policy

This application complies with the latest Google Play User Data and Permissions Policy:

  • The app only requests permissions necessary to provide core features clearly explained to users.
  • The app is completely ad-free and contains no advertising SDKs.
  • All data handling practices are transparent and follow Google Play’s Data Safety requirements.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically directed toward children under 13 years old, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.
